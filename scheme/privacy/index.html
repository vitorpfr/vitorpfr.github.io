Scheme

Privacy Policy

Last updated June 20, 2026

Scheme is a Home Screen personalization app for iOS. This policy explains what data Scheme accesses and — just as importantly — what it does not collect, store, or share.

Summary

Scheme does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. Everything Scheme does — theming, wallpaper generation, widget content — happens entirely on your device. There are no user accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

Calendar & Reminders

If you enable the Calendar or Reminders widgets, Scheme requests read-only access to your device's Calendar and/or Reminders (via Apple's EventKit framework) so it can display your upcoming events or due items directly in the widget. This data is read on-device only, is never transmitted anywhere, and is never stored outside of normal iOS app memory. You can revoke this access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.

Location (Weather)

Scheme includes an optional Weather widget that, when enabled, uses your device's location solely to fetch a local forecast for display. Location data is used on-device for this purpose only and is never stored or transmitted to any server operated by us.

Purchases

Scheme Pro purchases (subscriptions and one-time unlocks) are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never receive or store your payment details. Your purchase/entitlement state is verified locally on-device using Apple's transaction system.

No tracking, no third parties

Scheme does not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or any third-party data-sharing services. No data ever leaves your device through Scheme.

Children's privacy

Scheme is not directed at children under 13, and in any case does not collect personal information from any user, regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "last updated" date.
