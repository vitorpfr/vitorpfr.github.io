Common questions

  • Widgets aren't showing the latest data. iOS refreshes widgets on its own schedule, not instantly. Re-opening Scheme or backgrounding/foregrounding the app helps trigger a refresh sooner.
  • I bought Scheme Pro on another device — how do I restore it? Open Settings → Scheme Pro → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
  • An app I tapped on the launcher widget didn't open. Some apps don't support being opened this way, or may need to be reinstalled — try removing and re-adding the app from the picker.
